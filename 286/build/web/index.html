Welcome To oRiight

oRiight is small startup that specialises in geolocation of Mobile devices by keeping user's serial numbers in the database so that they can easily get them should the phone box be lost or damaged. We also keep Serial numbers for other devices like laptops, but tracking for such devices is not available yet, just keeping them for you.
